About Me

Background

I completed my MPhil in 2009 at the School of Mathematics and Computer Science, Fujian Normal University, under the supervision of Professor Weigang Su, following the receipt of my Bachelor's degree from Taishan College in 2006. I commenced my doctoral studies in 2014 at Sichuan Normal University's School of Mathematics and Software Science and was awarded a PhD in 2017 under the guidance of Professor Yiran He. My research was further advanced through a postdoctoral fellowship (2021-2023) at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University under Professor Ting Kei Pong, a role which was subsequently promoted to Research Fellow in 2023.
